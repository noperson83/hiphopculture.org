About LabMunkzInk

LabMunkzInk is an experiment in poetry, rhythm, and art. Hailing from Tucson, Arizona, this collective of artists is skilled in DJing, beat production, songwriting, and live performances.

Original members include Dr. Nope / Jesus / Marcus Lovato, St. Dominic (DJ Captain Antenna), and DJ Prado. Inspired by the foundational elements of hip-hop, the crew also brings a mobile studio so inspiration can become creation anywhere.

Dominic “DJ Captain Antenna” Capanna still mixes on vinyl, crafting unique sets with two turntables, a mixer, a microphone, and a looper. He can make music from thrift-store toys to traditional instruments.
